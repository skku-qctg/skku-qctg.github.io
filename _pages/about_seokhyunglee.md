## Seok-Hyung Lee

**Group Leader** · Assistant Professor

[Personal Website](https://seokhyung-lee.github.io) · [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=NURGJAwAAAAJ)

Seok-Hyung Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Quantum Information Engineering at SKKU. His research focuses on quantum error correction, including decoding algorithms, logical gate implementations, and fault-tolerant quantum computing with color codes and photonic systems.

**Education:**
- Ph.D. in Physics, Seoul National University (2023)
- B.S. in Physics, Seoul National University (2017)

**Previous Position:**
- Postdoc, University of Sydney (2023-2025)
